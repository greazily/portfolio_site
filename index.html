I’m a graphic designer currently based in Stockholm, with a background in graphic design from Middlesex University in London and interaction design from Stockholm University.

I started out at the London studio Stinsensqueeze, and now work independently with a focus on brand identity, motion design, web design and development, 3D graphics, and visual neural networks. Curiosity shapes every stage of my process—from first idea to final delivery. I make a point of understanding who the audience really is, what engages them and what they aspire to, often through unexpected research questions and avenues of lateral thinking—nothing's off the table in pursuit of a more meaningful concept and execution. In the end, it’s about crafting visual systems that speak with clarity and intention.
